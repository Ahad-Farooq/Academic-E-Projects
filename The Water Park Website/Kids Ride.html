Alligator Alley

Hold on for dear life as you attempt to walk across alligators (not real of course). Using the net above, test your balancing skills to walk across floating swamp animals…

Barefootin Bay

This new water play structure is packed with interactive features like sprayers, 3 slides and a giant Pineapple tipping bucket to make a super splash below.

Mushroom Pool

A bright and colourful pool with lots of splashing style water park fun! Ride the mini water slide, stomp around in the shallows or play with the squirting frogs! And as the name suggests – there are 2 magical giant mushrooms – cascading water from above – jump inside and hide – or splash out to surprise mum or dad.

Billabong Beach

A special character-filled themed area just for young children. Watch them play in shallow pools and take the challenge of the mini water slides, dodging jets of water from colourful, make-believe critters like Charlie the Croc, Percy the Pelican and Freddie Frog.

(Adult Supervision Required)
